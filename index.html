man.Renaissance

Data analyst and former account executive, converted developer.

In the first half of my life I obtained a B.B.A. from Middle Tennessee State Universty, majoring in Marketing. After 2.5 years with Dell inc., I decided to visit the furthest western culture possible, Australia. Returning to Nashville it was time to for another challenge in South Florida. A couple years in Florida will teach any person with enough sanity to solidify a long term personal vision and benchmarks to follow. So after much mental deliberation and enlightenment I've discovered Nashville Software School.
